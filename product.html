MERCEDES A35 AMG

type: Mb177

form USD 42.999 $

The Mercedes-Benz A35 AMG is a high-performance version of the standard A-Class compact luxury car. It features a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 302 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The car comes with a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission and all-wheel drive as standard. The A35 AMG also has sport-tuned suspension and brakes, as well as unique exterior and interior styling. It can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 4.7 seconds and has a top speed of 250 km/h. Other features include an upgraded infotainment system, sport seats, and a panoramic sunroof. It provides a perfect balance of luxury, sportiness and everyday practicality.
